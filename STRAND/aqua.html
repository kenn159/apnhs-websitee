WHAT IS AQUA STRAND?

The aquaculture strand not only focuses on theoretical knowledge but also emphasizes practical skills. The survey explored the practical skills that students expect to learn, including fish hatchery management, water quality monitoring, and aquatic health management.

AQUA GRADUATE JOBS

  • AQUACULTURE FARM MANAGER - Overseeing the operations of fish farms, managing staff, and ensuring the health of the aquatic species

  • AQUA TECHNICIAN - Assisting in the daily operations of fish farms, including feeding, monitoring water quality, and maintaining equipment.

  • AQUACULTURE EDUCATOR - Teaching about aquaculture practices in schools or community programs (often requires further education).

  • FISH FEED PRODUCTION OPERATOR - Manufacturing and testing fish feed to ensure nutritional quality.
