WHAT IS HUMANITIES AND SOCIAL SCIENCES?

The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand focuses on developing student's understanding of human behavior, society, and culture. It is part of the K-12 Curriculum in the Philippines, aiming to enhance critical thinking and analytical skills. HUMSS prepares students for careers in fields such as education, social work, journalism, and psychology, while also encouraging deeper insights into social issues and human experiences.
